HEAL YOUR WOUNDS

Mindfulness involves focusing your awareness on the present
It means paying attention to your sensations, feelings, thoughts,
and environment in the here-and-nowbr with an attitude of acceptance.
Some of the potential benefits of mindfulness include lowering stress,
decreasing depression, improving memory, and
strengthening your relationships, among other things.
